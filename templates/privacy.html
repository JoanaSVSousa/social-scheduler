Privacy Policy

Supernova Social Scheduler

Supernova stores only the account credentials and publishing metadata needed to schedule, verify, and publish posts to connected social platforms.

Connected Meta, Instagram, Facebook, and Threads credentials are used only to authenticate API requests selected by the account owner. Access tokens are stored securely by the application and are not sold or shared with third parties.

Users can remove connected social accounts from the API Accounts page. Removing an account deletes the stored publishing credentials for that platform from Supernova.

For privacy or data deletion requests, contact the account owner through the business contact email configured for this Meta app.
